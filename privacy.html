Bible Heroes Run — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Bible Heroes Run ("the App") is a children's educational game designed to teach Bible stories through gameplay. We take children's privacy seriously.

Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information. The App does not require:

  • Account creation
  • Email address
  • Name or age
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers

Data Storage

Game progress (level completion, stars earned, high scores) is stored locally on your device only using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Advertising

Bible Heroes Run contains NO advertisements of any kind. We will never show ads to children.

In-App Purchases

Bible Heroes Run contains NO in-app purchases. All 1000 levels are completely free.

Third Party Services

Bible Heroes Run uses no third party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Bible Heroes Run is designed for children and fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
