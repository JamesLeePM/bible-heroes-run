About

Bible Heroes Run is a free educational platformer game featuring 1000 levels teaching children Bible stories from Genesis to Revelation. No ads, no in-app purchases.

FAQ

Is the game free?

Yes — completely free, no ads, no purchases, all 1000 levels included.

What age is it for?

Ages 4 and up. Difficulty settings help younger children enjoy it too.

How do I use superpowers?

Tap the yellow lightning bolt button during gameplay!

My progress was lost

Progress saves locally on your device. Reinstalling the app may reset progress.
