About the Game

Bible Heroes Run is a free educational platformer game featuring 1000 levels that teach children about Bible stories from Genesis to Revelation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the game free?

Yes! Bible Heroes Run is completely free with no ads and no in-app purchases. All 1000 levels are included.

What age is it for?

Bible Heroes Run is designed for children ages 4 and up. Difficulty settings allow younger children to enjoy it too.

How do I unlock more levels?

Complete each level to unlock the next one. Collect scrolls and finish without damage to earn 3 stars!

How do I use character superpowers?

Tap the ⚡ yellow button during gameplay to activate your hero's unique superpower. Each Bible hero has a different special ability!

My progress was lost — what do I do?

Progress is saved locally on your device. If you reinstall the app, progress may be reset. We recommend noting your highest level reached.
